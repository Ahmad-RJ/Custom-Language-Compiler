Start → Stmt
Stmt → WhileStmt | Expr :: | IfStmt | CompStmt
WhileStmt → while (Rvalue) Stmt 
IfStmt → Agar (Rvalue) Stmt ElsePart 
ElsePart → Wargarna Stmt | ^ 
CompStmt → {StmtList} 
StmtList → Stmt StmtList' 
StrutList' → Stmt Stmtlist' | ^
RelOp → == | < | > | <= | >= != | <>
Expr → identifier := Mag | ^ 
Rvalue → Mag Rvalue' 
Rvalue' → RelOp Mag Rvalue' | ^ 
Mag → Term Mag' 
Mag' → + Term Mag' | - Term Mag' | ^
Term → Factor Term'
Term' → * Factor Term' | / Factor Term' | ^
Factor → (Mag) | identifier | number
